The Hidden Reality

Working Families

63% of food-insecure households include at least one working adult, showing that employment doesn't always guarantee food security.

Rural Communities

Rural areas face unique challenges with 87% higher rates of food insecurity compared to urban areas because of reasons like accessibility and affordability

Senior Citizens

More than 5.2 million seniors face food insecurity, often struggling with fixed incomes and choosing between medications and meals.
