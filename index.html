The Hidden Reality

Working Families

63% of food-insecure households include at least one working adult, highlighting that employment doesn't always guarantee food security.

Rural Communities

Senior Citizens

5.2 million seniors face food insecurity, often struggling with fixed incomes and choosing between medications and meals.
